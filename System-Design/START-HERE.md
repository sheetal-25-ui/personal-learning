# System Design — HLD + LLD

## What is System Design?

System Design = How to build large-scale applications that serve millions of users

### Two Parts

| Part | Full Name | What It Covers | Asked For |
|------|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| **HLD** | High Level Design | Architecture, databases, APIs, scaling | SDE-2, SDE-3, Senior |
| **LLD** | Low Level Design | Classes, interfaces, design patterns, OOP | SDE-1, SDE-2 |

---

## Who Needs What?

| Your Level | HLD | LLD |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| Fresher / 0-2 years | Basic understanding | Must know well |
| 3-5 years | Must know well | Must know well |
| 5+ years (Senior) | Deep expertise | Good understanding |

---

## 8-Week Plan

### LLD (Week 1-4)
| Week | Topics |
|------|--------|
| **1** | OOP concepts, SOLID principles, UML basics |
| **2** | Design Patterns (Creational + Structural) |
| **3** | Design Patterns (Behavioral) + Practice |
| **4** | LLD Interview Problems (Parking Lot, BookMyShow, etc.) |

### HLD (Week 5-8)
| Week | Topics |
|------|--------|
| **5** | Fundamentals: Load balancing, caching, databases, CDN |
| **6** | Deep dives: SQL vs NoSQL, message queues, microservices |
| **7** | Classic HLD problems (URL Shortener, Instagram, WhatsApp) |
| **8** | Microsoft-specific HLD problems + Mock |
