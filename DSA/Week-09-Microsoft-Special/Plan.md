## Microsoft Interview Tips

1. **Talk while you code** — explain your thinking out loud
2. **Start with brute force** — then optimize. Interviewers want to see your thought process
3. **Ask clarifying questions** — "Can the array have negatives?", "Is the input sorted?"
4. **Write clean code** — use meaningful variable names, not i/j/k everywhere
5. **Test your code** — walk through a small example after writing
6. **Know time/space complexity** — always state Big-O after solving
