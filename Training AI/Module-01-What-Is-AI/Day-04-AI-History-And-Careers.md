# Module 1 — Day 4: History of AI, Why Now, and Career Paths

## A Quick History of AI

| Year | What Happened | Why It Matters |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| **1950** | Alan Turing asks "Can machines think?" | The idea of AI is born |
| **1956** | The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined | AI becomes a field of study |
| **1966** | First chatbot (ELIZA) | Could have simple conversations |
| **1997** | IBM Deep Blue beats chess champion | AI beats humans at a specific task |
| **2011** | IBM Watson wins Jeopardy! | AI understands natural language questions |
| **2012** | Deep Learning revolution begins | Neural networks start beating everything |
| **2014** | GANs invented (Generative AI begins) | AI can CREATE images, not just classify |
| **2016** | AlphaGo beats Go world champion | AI conquers the "impossible" game |
| **2017** | Transformer architecture invented | The foundation for ChatGPT, Claude, etc. |
| **2020** | GPT-3 released | AI writes essays, code, poems |
| **2022** | ChatGPT launches | AI goes mainstream — 100M users in 2 months |
| **2023** | Claude, GPT-4, Gemini | AI becomes genuinely useful for work |
| **2024-26** | AI agents, coding AI, multimodal AI | AI does complex multi-step tasks |

## Why is AI Exploding NOW? (3 Reasons)

### 1. DATA Explosion
- In 2010: Humanity created 2 zettabytes of data per year
- In 2025: Over 120 zettabytes per year
- AI is hungry for data — and now we have MORE than enough

### 2. COMPUTING Power
- GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) turned out to be perfect for AI math
- Cloud computing (AWS, Google Cloud) made powerful computers available to everyone
- What took weeks in 2010 takes minutes today

### 3. ALGORITHMS Got Better
- The Transformer (2017) changed everything
- Better training techniques (like RLHF used by Claude)
- Open source community sharing breakthroughs freely

```
DATA + COMPUTING POWER + BETTER ALGORITHMS = AI BOOM
```
