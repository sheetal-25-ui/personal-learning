# Module 4: Math for AI — Overview

## Don't Worry! Only the Math You ACTUALLY Need.

You do NOT need to be a math genius for AI. You need to understand these concepts at an intuitive level — I'll explain them with pictures and examples, not complex formulas.

## 4 Days of Lessons

| Day | Topic | What You'll Learn |
|-----|-------|-------------------|
| **Day 01** | Linear Algebra Basics | Vectors, matrices, dot product — the language of AI |
| **Day 02** | Statistics for AI | Mean, median, variance, normal distribution, correlation |
| **Day 03** | Probability for AI | Basic probability, Bayes theorem, conditional probability |
| **Day 04** | Calculus Intuition | What is a derivative? What is gradient? (No complex formulas!) |

## Key Idea
- Linear Algebra = How AI stores and transforms data
- Statistics = How AI understands data
- Probability = How AI makes uncertain decisions
- Calculus = How AI learns (gradient descent)

You will understand WHY AI math works, not just memorize formulas.
